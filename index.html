About Us.

At Fixpoint Appliance and Plumbing, we provide expert residential appliance repair, installation, and maintenance, along with trusted plumbing services for homeowners. Our licensed and insured technicians are committed to delivering fast, reliable, and professional service—every time. Whether it's a broken washer, a new appliance installation, or a leaking pipe, we handle it all with care and precision. We offer honest pricing, same-day service when available, and always treat your home with respect. Your comfort and satisfaction are our top priorities. Contact us today through Angi—we’re ready to be your go-to home service experts for appliance and plumbing needs!
