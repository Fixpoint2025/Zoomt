About Us

At Fixpoint Appliance and Plumbing LLC, we specialize in fast, reliable, and professional appliance repair and plumbing services for homeowners who value quality and convenience. With years of hands-on experience, our certified technicians are committed to delivering top-notch service—whether it’s installing a new appliance, repairing a leaking faucet, or responding to an emergency plumbing issue.

  • Skilled and certified technicians with years of experience
  • Prompt, dependable service with clear communication
  • High-quality repairs and installations that last

We believe in honest work, clear communication, and long-term solutions that save you time and stress. Your comfort and satisfaction are our top priorities. Let us take the hassle out of home repairs—so you can get back to what matters most.
